Easily add your eEndorsements.com endorsements, testimonials, and reviews to your Wordpress web site or blog.

== Description ==

The eEndorsements plugin allows you to easily add your company's eEndorsements.com endorsements, testimonials, and reviews to your Wordpress web site or blog. Rather than having to copy and paste your reviews from various review sites around the web, you can collect them into your eEndorsements.com account and then simply use our plugin shortcode to display your reviews within pages or to create custom widgets.

== Installation ==

1. Go to your admin area and select Plugins -> Add new from the menu.
2. Search for "eEndorsements".
3. Click install.
4. Click activate.
5. Click settings to configure your API credentials.

== Configuration ==

Once you have configured your eEndorsements plugin, you can place a shortcode within any page, post, or widget where you wish for your endorsements to be displayed:

[eendorsements]
